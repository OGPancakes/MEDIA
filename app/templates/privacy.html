Politics In Action Social

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Politics In Action Social lets people create profiles, post text and media, send direct messages, and manage account settings. This page explains what information the app stores and how it is used.

Information we collect

When you create an account, the app stores the information you submit, such as your username, display name, email address, password hash, bio, and profile settings. If you upload photos or videos, those files are stored with your account content. If you send messages, posts, comments, reports, or poll votes, that information is also stored.

How we use information

Your information is used to run the service, display your profile and content, let you communicate with other users, support moderation, and maintain account security. App activity such as likes, reposts, bookmarks, notifications, and post views may also be stored to power app features.

Sharing

Profile information, posts, stories, and other content you choose to publish may be visible to other users depending on your privacy settings. Direct messages are intended for the participants in a conversation and authorized moderation or administrative review within the service.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account from the Settings screen inside the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile and associated app data stored by this service, subject to any limited retention required to investigate abuse or comply with legal obligations.

Contact

If you need help with privacy or account requests, use the support contact listed in the App Store submission materials or within your service documentation.
